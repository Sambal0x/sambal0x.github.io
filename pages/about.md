## Richard

**Lead Security Consultant — SilentGrid**

Richard is a Lead Security Consultant at **SilentGrid**, where he delivers penetration testing and red teaming services to clients across a wide range of industries, including aviation, finance, entertainment, retail, and government.

Previously, he was a Senior Security Consultant at **Trustwave SpiderLabs**, and prior to that worked as a penetration tester at **The Missing Link** and **Deloitte**, supporting Fortune 500 organizations across multiple sectors.

He brings over **10 years of combined experience** across engineering and information security, with deep expertise in offensive security, infrastructure, and enterprise environments.
